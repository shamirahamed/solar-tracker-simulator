About

This dashboard estimates daily solar tracker behaviour using location, date, geometry, and backtracking inputs.

Core calculations

  • Solar position from latitude, longitude, date, and timezone
  • Ideal tracker angle, limited angle, and backtracking angle
  • Shadow length and inter-row shading indication
  • Power and daily energy comparison
  • Ground Coverage Ratio (GCR)

Notes

  • 2D view may be visually compressed to fit smaller screens
  • Displayed values always come from the simulation result
  • Mobile rendering is for readability, not absolute scale drawing
Powered by pvlib-style solar calculations
