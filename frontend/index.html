About

This solar tracker simulator models a single-axis solar tracker system using location, date, and tracker configuration inputs.

Included calculations:

  • Solar position using latitude, longitude, date, and timezone
  • Ideal tracker angle, limited tracker angle, and backtracking angle
  • Shadow length and inter-row shading effect
  • Shading percentage with and without backtracking
  • Power output and daily energy comparison
  • Energy gain percentage due to backtracking
  • 2D tracker visualization with sun path and shadow direction

Powered by pvlib for solar position and tracker calculations, with FastAPI backend processing and frontend chart visualization.
