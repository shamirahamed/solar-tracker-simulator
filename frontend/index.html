About

About this tool

This simulator compares a single-axis tracker with and without backtracking using pvlib-based solar position, tracker angle, clear-sky irradiance, POA irradiance, and row shading logic.

What is visualized

Black line = ground shadow. Red section = inter-row shading only. Shadow visuals may be display-scaled for readability when low-sun shadows become very long.
